Hip-Hop:

Hip-Hop, also known as rap music or simply rap, is a genre of music that erupter in the 1970's. It is popular for break dancing and block parties in New York City. Some notable artists include Nicki Minaj, Snoop Dogg, Cardi B, and many others.

Hip-Hop combines elements of rhythm, blues, jazz, funk, and soul.

The first Hip-Hop artist is thought to be DJ Kool Herc, also known as the first rapper.
